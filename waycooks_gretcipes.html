Grr's Gourmet ExtravaGanza

Grr didn't work a traditional job this year - giving her a flexible schedule to plan, shop for, and cook more elaborate meals. Living in Seoul, it's easy to get around on public transpo - but given all that stuff we don't need to address, you can understand that she wasn't very eager to get on the subway or bus. Instead, she learned to longboard, and took her wheels 2km to the supermarket and back with a backpack full of pork shoulder and olive oil, or made the 8km trek to Costco to load up on cheese and wine.
