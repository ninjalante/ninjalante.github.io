New Year Challenges

May 26, 2021

We know, okay? We know what the date is. But does it really feel like it's been five months since Christmas???


In January, A-Game issued a yearlong challenge: have a food goal for the year. Things like cooking more, getting takeout less, and using up pantry items. Kat B, who eats a lot of frozen chicken nuggets and Kraft Dinner, decided that 2021 was the year to eat more vegetables. It's a noble goal, and she's left us all in the dust.

  1. Preppin' Veggies
  2. Avocado Salad
  3. Chopped Spinach Salad
  4. Breakfast Bokeumbap
  5. Pasta alla Broccolini

After all that fiber, what's some chicky nuggies and mac & cheese gonna hurt?

Congrats to our dear Kat B for eating so well this year!
