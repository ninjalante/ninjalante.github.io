Book an audition through the Google form found here

If you are not available for audition weekend, email seoulplayers@gmail.com to submit a virtual audition.

Attending in person is preferred -- it gives you and the directors a chance to interact!

On audition day, show up 5-10 minutes before your assigned time. You will receive sides to perform with the other actors in your time slot. Directors may request to see you in a specific scene with another actor.

The more you show us, the more roles you may be considered for! There are sixteen plays being cast. Show your range!

Make bold choices. We love to see them~
